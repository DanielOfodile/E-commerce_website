E-commerce Portfolio Project

Introduction

Our E-commerce portfolio project was a challenging and rewarding experience for our team. We set out to create an online marketplace for small businesses to sell their products, with a focus on simplicity and ease of use for both the business owners and shoppers.

The team consisted of three members: Bright as a frontend developer, Daniel as the project fullstack engineer, and John as a backend developer. We worked together over a period of three weeks to bring our vision to life.

The Story

The inspiration for this project came from our personal experiences shopping at small, local businesses. We wanted to create a platform that would make it easy for these businesses to reach a wider audience and grow their sales post Covid-19 lockdown. Our goal was to create a user-friendly and visually appealing platform that would make the shopping experience enjoyable for customers.

Accomplishments

Our E-commerce portfolio project was built using Python, Django and Bootstrap. We chose these technologies because of their flexibility and scalability, as well as their large and supportive communities.

One of the main accomplishments of our project was the creation of a fully functional and visually appealing e-commerce platform. The platform was designed with a user-centered approach, and we paid close attention to the user experience to ensure that it was easy to navigate and understand.

The website featured a variety of e-commerce functionalities such as the ability for customers to browse and search for products, view product details, add items to their cart, and proceed to checkout.

Another notable feature of the website was the ability for business owners to easily manage their products, orders and inventory. The admin interface was simple and intuitive, allowing them to easily add and update products, view orders and manage stock.

Additionally, we used Bootstrap to give the website a modern and responsive design. This ensured that the website was optimized for different screen sizes and devices, providing an optimal viewing experience for users on any device.

In summary, our project was built using Python, Django and Bootstrap, which allowed us to create a fully functional and visually appealing e-commerce platform. The website featured a variety of e-commerce functionalities and was designed with a user-centered approach, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for customers.

Technical Challenges

One of the most difficult challenges we faced was implementing a secure payment system. We had to ensure that sensitive customer information was protected and comply with industry standards for online transactions. This required a significant amount of research and testing, and giving the scope of our mvp and the development period for this project, we opted to provide a checkout form to ensure that we were able to provide a secure and reliable checkout process for our customers.

Learnings and Takeaways

Working on this project taught us a lot about ourselves as engineers and about the importance of user-centered design. It also reinforced the importance of security and compliance in online transactions. It has been a great learning experience and we hope to continue working on similar projects in the future.

About the Team

Our team consisted of three members, Bright, Daniel, and John. Bright served as the designer, Daniel the project manager, and John the lead backend developer. All of us have different background and skillsets, but we all had a shared passion for creating a user-friendly and visually appealing e-commerce platform for small businesses.
